MUCOSITY: Reign of the Molds is a first-person shooter game currently in development for Windows and Linux.



In this game, you play as a character in a world overrun by the Molds—robotic weapons with bodies made from genetically engineered slime molds, built by the evil Goof-Lord. You'll have to defeat them if you want to rescue the world from its impending destruction.
